Hello! I'm Akindu Bandara, a dedicated student of Computer Science at the University of Westminster. I hail from the vibrant city of Panadura in Sri Lanka, where my lifelong passion for technology first blossomed. This passion has steered me toward a promising career path focused on Software Development, Cybersecurity, and Data Science.

From a young age, I was fascinated by the mechanisms behind our modern conveniences and the software that powers them. My academic journey in computer science is not just a pursuit of knowledge but a mission to make the world a more accessible and efficient place for everyone.
